    Story of the day

    The Classic Lorient began with an early break of eight riders. From that move, three escaped, including Lucinda Brand. After numerous attacks, the race regrouped for a sprint. Mischa Bredewold launched early and held off Marianne Vos to take victory. It was Bredewold’s third consecutive win in this race, confirming her dominance in this race.
