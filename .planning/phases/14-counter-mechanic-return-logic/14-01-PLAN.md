<objective>
Remove the bounce-back (blowback) mechanic from the counter system. When an attack order (shimanobil, covid, bondestreik) is countered by a defense order (etappeseier, blodpose_gt), the attack simply has no effect and is returned to the attacker for future reuse. No penalty is applied to the attacker.

Purpose: The 2026 ruleset changes the counter mechanic from "bounce-back effect on attacker" to "order returned for reuse with no effect on anyone". This simplifies scoring and makes counters less punishing.

Output: Updated counter resolution logic, cleaned-up types, and updated UI descriptions.
</objective>
